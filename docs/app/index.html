Sidechannel Reports

Although VPNs encrypt all your traffic, it can still be possible to infer information.

These are refered to as sidechannel vulnerabilities and can anything from detect visiting a slow site to determining the length of your ssh password

Sidekick gives reports on the detection of such vulnerabilities, so you are aware of them and can work to fix them
